# Gemini Genesis

Gemini Genesis is an AI-powered Career OS designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world placement readiness. It provides a unified platform for resume analysis, interview preparation, skill training, and performance monitoringâ€”built to scale for institutions and effective for individual learners.

## Problem Statement
A large percentage of engineering graduates underperform in interviews due to lack of practical preparation, absence of real-time feedback, and limited mentoring capacity within institutions.

## Solution Overview
Gemini Genesis acts as a 24/7 AI mentor for students and a centralized command center for educators. It combines interview simulations, live coding practice, ATS-based resume analysis, and structured skill training into a single enterprise-grade dashboard.

## Key Features
- **Executive Dashboard**: Real-time readiness scores, progress tracking, and target company planning.
- **Interview Orchestration Hub**:
  - AI video mock interviews
  - Live coding arena with AI assistance
  - Deep ATS-based resume scanning
- **Training Accelerator**: Three-level skill development system with AI English coaching and career mentoring.
- **Classroom Command Center**: Faculty view to monitor performance and identify students needing intervention.
- **Live AI Copilot**: Sidebar-based assistant for instant, contextual support.

## Technology Stack
- Google Gemini 2.5 Flash (core AI engine)
- Firebase Firestore (real-time data sync)
- Google Cloud Storage (secure document storage)
- Google Text-to-Speech (AI interviewer voice)
- Python-based data and logic layer

## High-Level Workflow
1. User logs in and views personalized dashboard.
2. Completes training modules and practice tasks.
3. Enters interview simulations and coding challenges.
4. AI analyzes performance and updates readiness metrics.
5. Educators monitor progress through analytics dashboards.

## Future Enhancements
- Real-time AI voice interaction
- Browser-based AI copilot extension
- Dedicated hiring portals for companies
